🦅 Set Up Your Profile

Tell JobHawk what you're looking for. This takes 2 minutes and runs on autopilot after.

1. What roles are you targeting?

One per line or comma-separated. JobHawk searches for these specifically.

2. Where are you looking?

Select your location — JobHawk only applies to jobs in your area. Remote jobs are always included regardless of location.

3. Additional keywords (optional)

Skills, industry terms, or tools that should boost a job's match score.

4. Terms to exclude (optional)

Jobs containing these words will be skipped.

5. Your email for sending job applications (optional)

When JobHawk finds a contact email inside a job posting, it sends an application from your email address directly to that employer. This is separate from your scan-result notifications — those come from the platform.

For Gmail, use an App Password (not your regular password). Leave blank to skip auto-email applications.

Advanced SMTP settings

6. Minimum match score to apply

Jobs below this score are tracked but not applied to automatically. Lower = more applications. Recommended: 10–20.

{{ profile.min_score or 10 }}

7. Scan-result email notifications

After every scan, JobHawk can email you a summary of jobs found and applications submitted. Uses your email credentials above.

You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any notification email, or uncheck this box.

You can change any of this later from your dashboard settings.
